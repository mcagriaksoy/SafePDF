Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2025

What are cookies?

Cookies and similar technologies (local storage) help the site remember choices and optionally collect anonymized usage metrics.

Cookie categories used

  • Necessary: Essential UI state (cookie banner state, modal open/close). These are stored locally and required for basic functionality.
  • Analytics (optional): Aggregated, anonymized usage metrics to help improve the documentation and downloads. Enabled only after user consent.
  • Marketing (optional): Third‑party cookies used for marketing integrations if explicitly enabled by you.

How we store preferences

Cookie preferences are stored in your browser using `localStorage` under the key `safepdf_cookie_prefs`. These preferences are never sent to an external service by default.

Purchases and third‑party checkout

If you purchase Pro features or other paid services, payment checkouts are handled by third‑party providers. During a purchase you may be redirected to an external checkout page that can set cookies; those cookies are governed by the payment provider's cookie policy. We do not transfer your cookie preferences to payment processors unless you explicitly opt in to related services.

Managing cookies

Use the Cookie Settings on the site or the footer link to change your preferences. You may also clear cookies and local storage via your browser to reset choices.

Opting out

If you reject analytics and marketing cookies, no optional trackers will run. Necessary functionality is unaffected.

Third‑party cookies

If you enable marketing or other third‑party features, those services may set cookies; review their privacy pages for details.
